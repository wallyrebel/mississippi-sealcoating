Our Process

How We Protect Your Mississippi Asphalt

01

Inspection & Assessment

We thoroughly inspect your Mississippi asphalt driveway or parking lot, identifying all cracks, damage, and problem areas. You receive a detailed assessment and transparent quote with no hidden fees.

02

Surface Preparation

Proper prep is the key to lasting results. We power-wash the entire surface, remove all dirt, debris, oil stains, and vegetation. Edges are trimmed and the surface is thoroughly cleaned.

03

Crack Filling & Repairs

All cracks are filled with hot-pour rubberized sealant. Potholes and damaged areas are patched with premium hot-mix asphalt and properly compacted for a smooth, even surface.

04

Premium Sealcoat Application

Two coats of commercial-grade sealer are applied using professional spray equipment for uniform coverage. We ensure complete edge-to-edge protection with a beautiful, rich black finish.

05

Final Inspection & Cure

We perform a thorough final inspection, barricade the area, and provide cure-time guidance. Your surface is ready for traffic in 24-48 hours with full protection that lasts for years.

Why Sealcoating?

The Science Behind Asphalt Protection

UV Protection

Mississippi's intense sun breaks down asphalt binder, causing oxidation, fading, and brittleness. Sealcoating blocks harmful UV rays, keeping your asphalt flexible and resilient.

Water Resistance

Mississippi receives 50-65 inches of rain annually. Water seeps into unsealed cracks, eroding the base and causing structural failure. Sealcoating creates an impermeable waterproof barrier.

Chemical Resistance

Oil, gasoline, and automotive fluids dissolve asphalt binder on contact. Sealcoating acts as a chemical shield, preventing these substances from penetrating and softening your pavement.

Cost Savings

Sealcoating costs pennies per square foot compared to dollars for repaving. Regular maintenance saves Mississippi property owners up to 75% over the life of their asphalt surface.

Curb Appeal

Nothing looks better than a freshly sealed, jet-black asphalt surface. Sealcoating instantly enhances the appearance of any Mississippi property, whether residential or commercial.

Longevity

Unsealed asphalt typically lasts 10-15 years. With regular sealcoating every 2-3 years, your Mississippi asphalt driveway or parking lot can last 25-30+ years, maximizing your investment.
